## Task: Market Positioning

Create a positioning framework for this product:

1. **Value Proposition Canvas**
   - Customer jobs-to-be-done
   - Pains they experience today
   - Gains they want
   - How our product addresses each

2. **Positioning Statement**
   - For [target customer]
   - Who [statement of need]
   - [Product name] is a [category]
   - That [key benefit]
   - Unlike [competitors]
   - Our product [key differentiator]

3. **Messaging Framework**
   - Headline (one sentence)
   - Subheadline (supporting detail)
   - 3 key benefits with proof points
   - Objection handling (top 3 objections + responses)

4. **Competitive Positioning Map**
   - Pick 2 axes that matter to customers
   - Plot competitors and our product
   - Identify the open quadrant we can own
