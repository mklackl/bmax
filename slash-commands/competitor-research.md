## Task: Competitor Research

Conduct a structured competitor analysis for this project. For each competitor found:

1. **Product**: Name, URL, one-line description
2. **Pricing**: Free tier? Price points? Usage-based or flat?
3. **Features**: Core features, what they do well, what they lack
4. **Positioning**: Who do they target? What's their messaging?
5. **Weaknesses**: Bad reviews, missing features, poor UX, pricing complaints
6. **Market signals**: Review count, social mentions, estimated traffic/users if available

Then synthesize:
- **Market gaps**: What are competitors NOT doing that users want?
- **Differentiation opportunities**: Where can we win?
- **Pricing insight**: What's the expected price range for this category?
- **Risk assessment**: Is this market too crowded, or is there room?

Output as a structured markdown document in `_bmad-output/planning-artifacts/competitor-research.md`.
