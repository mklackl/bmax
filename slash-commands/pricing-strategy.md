## Task: Pricing Strategy

Design a pricing strategy for this SaaS product:

1. **Pricing Model Selection**
   - Evaluate: Freemium, flat-rate, tiered, usage-based, per-seat, hybrid
   - Recommend one model with rationale
   - Define what's free vs paid (if freemium)

2. **Tier Design** (if tiered)
   - Free / Starter / Pro / Enterprise (or fewer)
   - Features per tier (feature gating matrix)
   - Price points with justification
   - Annual vs monthly pricing (discount strategy)

3. **Monetization Triggers**
   - What makes a free user convert?
   - What makes a Pro user upgrade?
   - Usage limits that drive upgrades without frustrating users

4. **Competitive Pricing Context**
   - What do competitors charge?
   - Where should we position (cheaper, same, premium)?
   - Price anchoring strategy

5. **Revenue Projections** (simple)
   - Target: X users at Y conversion rate = Z MRR
   - Break-even calculation if costs are known

Output as `_bmad-output/planning-artifacts/pricing-strategy.md`.
